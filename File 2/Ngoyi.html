Brief History of Lilian NgoyiLilian

Masedeba Ngoyi was born in Pretoria in 1911 to a Bapedi family of six children. She initially did teacher training at the Kilnerton Training Instituiton. When her father could no longer afford the fees, she began training as a nurse ar City Deep mine hospital. Ultimately, she took employment in a garment factory between 1945 andf 1956, where she joined and became active in the Garment Workers Union, umder the leadership of Solly Sachs.
